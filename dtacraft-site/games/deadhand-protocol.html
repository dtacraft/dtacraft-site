Game Profile

Deadhand Protocol

Single-player tactical card campaign in a post-network megacity. Build hybrid decks, adapt to enemy AI patterns, and survive escalating operation chains.

Core Loop

Scout mission data, tune your deck, execute high-risk runs, and recover rare fragments to unlock late-game archetypes.

Platforms

PC and mobile. Controller and touch interfaces planned in parallel from pre-production.

Status

Pre-alpha balancing phase. Weekly system notes are published on the News page.
