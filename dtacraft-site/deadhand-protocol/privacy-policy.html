Deadhand Protocol Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 31/12/2025

Deadhand Protocol ("the Game") is developed and published by the developer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Game.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Deadhand Protocol is an offline, single-player game.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Contacts or photos
  • Payment or financial information

2. Game Data

All game progress, settings, and unlocked content are stored locally on your device only.

This data is not transmitted to any server and cannot be accessed by the developer.

3. Third-Party Services

Deadhand Protocol does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud saves
  • Third-party SDKs that collect user data

4. Children’s Privacy

Deadhand Protocol does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the Game complies with applicable child privacy regulations.

5. Data Security

Because the Game does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of personal data leakage from our systems.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the Game’s features change. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer at:

contact@dtacraft.com
