DTA Craft Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 February 2026

1. Introduction

DTA Craft is an independent game development studio.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to our studio presence, website (if applicable), and games published under the DTA Craft name.

By accessing our content, pages, or games, you acknowledge this policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

DTA Craft does not directly collect, store, or sell personal data through this page or our games unless explicitly stated.

Our premium games are designed to operate without advertising networks or third-party behavioral tracking.

We do not sell user data to third parties.

3. Platform-Level Data Collection

Games published by DTA Craft may be distributed through third-party platforms such as Google Play.

These platforms may independently collect certain technical or transactional data (such as purchase confirmations, crash reports, or device information) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We do not control how platform providers collect or process such data.

Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of the respective distribution platforms.

4. In-App Purchases

If a game includes in-app purchases, all transactions are processed by the platform provider (e.g., Google Play, Itch.io).

DTA Craft does not receive or store payment details such as credit card information.

5. Direct Communication

If you contact DTA Craft via email, social media, or messaging platforms:

  • We may retain your message for the purpose of responding.
  • We do not share your contact information with third parties.
  • Communication data is used solely for support or inquiry purposes.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information voluntarily provided through direct communication.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed toward children under the age of 13 unless explicitly stated.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries:
