Terminal Bastion

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Terminal Bastion is a premium single-player game developed and published by DTA Craft.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the game.

1. Information We Collect

Terminal Bastion does not collect personal information.

The game:

  • does not collect personal data
  • does not require account registration
  • does not use analytics services
  • does not use advertising networks
  • does not track user behavior

All gameplay occurs locally on your device.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Terminal Bastion may store limited gameplay information locally on your device, such as:

  • game settings
  • progress or save data
  • unlocked content

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. Internet Access

Terminal Bastion does not require an internet connection to function.

If you download updates through the Google Play Store, the Google Play platform may collect information according to its own privacy policies.

You can review Google Play’s privacy policy here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children’s Privacy

Terminal Bastion does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Since the game does not collect or process personal data, it is generally safe for players of all ages.

However, distribution platforms such as Google Play may apply their own policies regarding child-directed applications.

5. Third-Party Services

Terminal Bastion does not integrate any third-party services, including:

  • analytics platforms
  • advertising networks
  • user tracking services
  • external login systems

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

If changes are made, the updated version will be published on the official DTA Craft website.
