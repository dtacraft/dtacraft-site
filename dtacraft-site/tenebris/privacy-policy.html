TENEBRIS

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28/02/2026

TENEBRIS (“the Game”) is developed and published by the developer. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Game.

1. No Personal Data Collection

TENEBRIS does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The Game does not require:

  • Account registration
  • Login
  • Internet connection
  • Cloud storage

The developer does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising ID
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos

2. Local Game Data

Game progress, puzzle completion data, and settings are stored locally on your device only.

This data:

  • Is not transmitted to the developer
  • Is not uploaded to any server
  • Is removed if you uninstall the Game

3. No Advertising

TENEBRIS does not display advertisements.

The Game does not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs.

No advertising identifiers or ad-related data are collected.

4. No In-App Purchases

TENEBRIS does not contain in-app purchases.

The Game is a one-time paid product distributed through:

  • Google Play

All payment processing is handled directly by Google Play.

The developer does not receive or store any payment information.

5. No Analytics

TENEBRIS does not use analytics services.

The Game does not collect:

  • Usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Behavioral tracking data

6. Children’s Privacy

Because TENEBRIS does not collect any personal data, it does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).

7. Data Security

The developer does not operate servers or databases for TENEBRIS.

All data remains on the user’s device.

8. Changes to This Policy

If the Game’s features change in the future (for example, adding online services), this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Any updates will be reflected on this page.
